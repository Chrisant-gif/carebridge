# CareBridge Vision

## Mission

CareBridge is an open-source NGO Management Platform designed to help nonprofit organizations efficiently manage beneficiaries, volunteers, donors, projects, events, and community impact.

Our mission is to make technology accessible to organizations that are changing lives.

## Vision

To become the most trusted and user-friendly management platform for NGOs across Africa and beyond.

## First Organization

Kingdom Caregivers

## Core Values

- Transparency
- Accountability
- Simplicity
- Accessibility
- Community
- Impact
